This website is for the waffles!

Fun fact!:Belgian waffles gained global fame after the 1964 New York World's Fair, where they were rebranded from "Brussels Waffles" because Americans didn't know where Brussels was, becoming a symbol of Belgian cuisine. There are two main types: lighter, crispier Brussels waffles and sweeter, denser Liège waffles with caramelized pearl sugar.

Belgian waffles

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

Step 1. Gather all ingredients.

Step 2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.

Step 3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.

step 4. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.

Step 5. Ladle batter into a preheated waffle iron

Step 6. Ladle batter into a preheated waffle iron

Step 7.And your done. Enjoy!
